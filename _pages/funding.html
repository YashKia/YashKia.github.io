Current Grants

Thrasher Research Fund Early Career Award (2024-2027)

Role: Principal Investigator

Title: "Enhancing Behavioral Understandings and Interventions in Children with Autism Spectrum Disorder using Artificial Intelligence"

Amount: $25,000 per year

Description: This project applies artificial intelligence and machine learning techniques to analyze sleep patterns and develop interventions for children with autism spectrum disorder. The research aims to create predictive models that can forecast challenging behaviors and help caregivers implement preventative strategies.
